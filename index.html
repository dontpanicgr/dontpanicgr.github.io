Brief History

For the past 13 years i worked in some of the most interesting and challenging projects.

Started as freelancer back in 2003 building websites on top of Joomla & Wordpress, got hired as a Visual Designer in a small print studio in 2007 where i crafted all sort of things (from books to catalog or laser cut souvenirs) and setled down for 2 years in a pharmaceutical company as a Warehouse Manager and Designer in 2011.

After a 6-month runaway, trying to launch my own startup in 2014 and failing :) i found my place at PeoplePerHour.com.


What's next?

There is only one thing certain; my will to learn more, experience more, fail more and design more.
